About me

I grew up in the most northern part of Norway surrounded by nature and horses. I enjoyed doing photography as a child, which sparked my interest in design and technology later on. I learned to code at the age of 10, and when I was 19 I started my first job as a web developer after receiving my craft certificate in graphic design.

Since then I have been working as a designer/developer in several agencies in both Oslo and Northern Norway, and 2021 I started my 5 year masters degree in design at The Oslo School of Architecture and Design.
