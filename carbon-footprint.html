Carbon footprint

Did you know that the Internet requires a huge amount of electricity, which in turn leads to immense carbon emissions? According to websitecarbon.com the average website produces about 0,5 gram of CO2 per page view. For a website with 5000 monthly page views, that is about 105 kilos CO2 every year.
